About Me

With 20 years of experience as a high school math teacher, and passion for problem-solving, I am pivoting into the field of data science. With a Bachelors in Mathematics I am equipped with a deep understanding of mathematical principles and a desire to use data to drive decision-making, I bring a unique perspective to this rapidly-evolving industry.

With my strong analytical skills and ability to communicate complex concepts clearly and concisely. I am well-equipped to help organizations make sense of their data and leverage it to achieve their goals. Whether it's developing models to predict customer behavior, identifying trends to inform business strategy, or uncovering insights to drive innovation.

My passion for data science is rooted in a belief that data can be a powerful tool for good, helping organizations make smarter, more informed decisions that positively impact their bottom line and the world at large. With 20 years experience as a high school math teacher and my newfound skills in data science, I am excited to make a difference in this exciting and dynamic field. .
